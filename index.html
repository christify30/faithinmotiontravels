INTIMACY RETREAT

Have you been yearning for more of God? Do you desire to be part of a community of godly women? Has the year been overwhelming? Join a handful of other amazing women on a journey to discovering God away from the noise, cares and distractions of this world! From our Prayer walks to Bonding time , to games, photoshoot, work-out, Meet & Greet, Activation sessions, God talk, Girl time, Quiet time, etc at a fun destination in Lagos Nigeria, we are going to have it all at out first all girls trip/retreat!

WHAT IS THE INTIMACY RETREAT?

It has been a very distracting year. From news reports to health updates to closing churches, a lot of us have lost touch with God.

Hence, we want to make sure we end the year right by reflecting & reconnecting and start a new year from being inspired by the Holy Spirit.

If you have been seeking for that closeness with God, at the INTIMACY RETREAT, that bond will be created or restored. What’s more? The retreat has been put together to help each and everyone obtain clarity and forge ahead with purpose.

Activities

Here is a list of exciting activities lined up for the program

One hour with Jesus

Get to spend one hour of undistracted personal time with Jesus. Journal and pen will be provided to write down the special things God will say to you.

Word sessions

We would be digging deep into the word of God for treasures that will help us renew our minds and strengthen our faith.

Prayer sessions

The power of corporate prayer can never be overestimated. There is something incredibly special about God’s women gathering to touch and agree in prayer as concerning anything. Sisters that pray together stay together!

Prayer walks

Every Morning between 5:30-6:00am, we would take a 30minute group-walk around the neighbourhood praying in the holyGhost and communicating in the language of the Spirit to recharge/refuel our Spirit man.

Counselling sessions

(Optional) For those who need someone to talk to or a shoulder to cry on, Regina will be available to walk you through the path of offering godly counsel to help you navigate through any difficult season you are currently going through in your life.

Activation sessions

Very similar to corporate prayer time, we are going to be spending some time praying and activating the gifts of the spirit that have been lying dormant on our inside.

Girl time

Nothing like the famous girl chit-chats! We'd definitely make time to have round-circle sharing time where we get to bare it all, share it all and connect on a much more deeper level.

Testimony sessions

What has the Lord done for you? We all want to hear all about it!

DAILY ROUTINE

The daily routine for the program are as follows

  • Day 01
  • Day 02
  • Day 03

The first day commences as guests arrive and are welcomed by Faith-in-motion Travels staff. They are checked into their rooms followed by a welcome dinner.

The second day kicks off with a fast and spiritual detox featuring prayer walks, worship, the word, activation session, fruit lunch and a sumptuous night dinner. This day would be topped off by a girls’ time.

Day 3 features key insights from the retreats, professional photo shoots, delicious breakfast and holding of hands to share Graces

ABOUT FAITH IN MOTION TRAVELS

Co-founded by Regina Uzoma and IB, Faith In Motion Travels is a faith based travel company committed to the unique work of organizing faith inspired female retreats across the globe that creates a safe space for women of all ages and walks of life to unite, connect and seek the face of God individually and collectively, doing so uninhibited by the immediate distractions present in their lives. It is designed to also enable them obtain clarity for their lives in the secret place and forge ahead with their purpose.

The core focus of our retreats is to help women experience and enjoy quality time with God, connect with other godly women as well as rest and relax in the most beautiful, breathtaking and serene spots all over the world.

The Faith In Motion Travels was birthed as a result of the strong burden to help women seek the face of Jesus in the most intimate, unhindered way as well as help participants create lovely memories with the most amazing community of God-loving women/sisterhood. Our retreats and trips are designed to be spiritually rejuvenating, soul invigorating, and bodily exhilarating!
